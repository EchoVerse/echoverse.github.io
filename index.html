Game Design

EchoVerse is a collection of 5555 unique islands with endless possibilities. We invite you to your private island getaway whenever and wherever you want — A third-person relaxing simulation role-play game. You may explore the island — decorate your island during the day and enjoy the sunset on the beach. You may also share and show off your island to your friends or visit theirs. Check out the trailer and sneak peeks below!

Decorate

You may decorate the island with flowers and bushes, and add furniture to your house. You can walk on the island in your favorite 3D NFT character.

Social

You can throw parties on your island with awesome music and collect exclusive resources with your friends. Share and show off your island to them!

Earn

You’ll earn token rewards from staking your island. A fully decorated island will give you a higher staking rate. You can also rent out the island and collect rents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which blockchain?

The 5555 genesis islands will be minted on the Ethereum mainchain.

What is an island?

Each island is represented as an NFT. It contains a random combination of appearances and natural resources.

When is the game available?

The game will be built step by step. The first playable version will be released by November.
