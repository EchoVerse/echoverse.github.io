Game Design

Fish and plant items on the island
Attach an item (e.g. house models, trees, statues) to the island
Rent out the property to a certain wallet address (the “rentee”) to collect rent and resources bonuses

Changeable

You can shape the land by building a house of all kinds and growing a tree of your choice.

Exploitable

You acquire resources within the border of the land.

Profitable

You can rent out your property and collect rents.

Frequently Aksed Questions

Which blockchain?

The 5555 genesis islands will be minted on the Ethereum mainchain.

What is an island?

Each island is represented as an NFT. It contains a random combination of appearances and natural resources.

When is the game available?

The game will be built step by step. The first playable version will be released by November.
